Contact Us

So you want to get in touch...

So you saw something on the site you liked... or maybe someone you know shot you the link and suggested taking a look. imapex is only as strong as our members, and we are always looking to add new SE Developers to our ranks.

imapex leverages Cisco Spark for all communication. Email just feels so old and outdated, not something a modern development team would use :-). Just provide your email address and tick the appropriate boxes and Dave the Reindeer will add you to the key rooms where our members are ready to get you started.

imapex membership is currently limited to Cisco SEs at this time. If you are a customer, partner, or student please feel free to ask questions and chat over Spark, as well as leverage the demos and education resources available on the site.
